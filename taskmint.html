About the App

What is TaskMint?

TaskMint is an Android app that lets users in India earn real money by completing simple tasks on their smartphones. Unlike survey-only apps or apps that pay in non-withdrawable gift cards, TaskMint pays in points that convert directly into Indian Rupees — withdrawable instantly via UPI or within 1–2 days via bank transfer.

Users can earn through multiple channels: completing sponsored tasks (installing apps, signing up for services, completing KYC), playing daily games like Spin & Win and Scratch & Win, watching short videos, taking surveys, and checking in daily for streak bonuses.

The referral system lets users grow their earnings passively — earn ₹10 instantly when a friend joins, plus ₹20 for every friend who completes their first task. The wallet dashboard shows real-time balance, transaction history, and withdrawal status at all times.

TaskMint was designed, built, and is maintained entirely by Altaf Ansari under Altaf Tech — committed to building products that deliver real value to users.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Is TaskMint free to download?+
Yes, TaskMint is completely free to download and use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.
How do I withdraw my earnings?+
Go to the Wallet section, select Withdraw, choose UPI (instant) or Bank Transfer (1–2 days), enter your details, and submit. Minimum withdrawal is ₹10 (1000 pts).
How many points equal ₹1?+
100 points = ₹1. So ₹10 requires 1000 pts, ₹50 requires 5000 pts, and so on.
How many tasks can I complete per day?+
Sponsored tasks vary by availability. Daily games (Spin, Scratch, Watch, Surveys, Quizzes, Visit) each allow 5 attempts per day. Daily check-in is once per day.
Is this available outside India?+
Currently TaskMint is designed for Indian users as withdrawals are via UPI and Indian bank accounts. International support may be added in future updates.
How does the referral system work?+
Share your unique referral code. When a friend signs up using your code, you earn ₹10 instantly. When they complete their first task, you earn an additional ₹20.
My withdrawal is pending — what should I do?+
UPI withdrawals process instantly but may take a few hours during peak times. Bank transfers take 1–2 business days. If still pending beyond that, email us at taskmint93@gmail.com.
